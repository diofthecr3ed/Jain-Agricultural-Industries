About Us

Established in 1968, JAIN AGRICULTURAL INDUSTRIES is a privately owned firm and the leading manufacturers of Axles & Axle-ENDS with or without brake system for A.D.V., any type of Trollies, Tankers, Carts/Carriers, Agricultural Machinery (such as Threashers) and Earthmoving equipments. With an experience of about 57 years, our company has attained wide-ranging know how in developing, manufacturing and marketing the products with 100% customer satisfaction.
The management, production & sales team co-ordinates effectively to provide the best output with their unparalleled experience, technical know-how, deep knowledge in manufacturing process and expertise in selling the products.
Currently, we are serving in the Indian market under the brand name “GOEL” and focusing on to take “GOEL” brand globally to fulfil the aspirations of our clients. To increase the demand of our customers and to compete in the market we are supplying wheels/discs/rims for A.D.V., any type of Trollies, Tankers, Carts/Carriers, Agricultural Machinery (such as Threashers) and Earthmoving equipments. We outsource our wheels/discs/rims under our stringent supervision and ensure that each product matches the standards of our “GOEL” brand name.
In this competitive time we are providing appropriate & alternative solutions to the clients who are looking for axles and wheels for their machines, carriers or any other Earthmoving equipments by maintaining high quality, technical competence, and selection of finest raw material. Accuracy, Commitment, Consistency & Premium Quality is the four pillars of our Company.
